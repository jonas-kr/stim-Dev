Agreement Details

  • Data Confidentiality

    • The agency agrees to keep all client data confidential and not disclose it to third parties without prior authorization.

  • Data Security Measures

    • The agency will implement appropriate security measures to protect client data from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

  • Use of Data

    • Client data will only be used for the purposes specified in the agreement and will not be used for any other purposes without explicit consent.

  • Data Ownership

    • The client retains ownership of all data provided to the agency and any intellectual property rights associated with it.

  • Data Retention

    • The agency will only retain client data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in the agreement or as required by law.

  • Data Transfer

    • If data transfer is necessary, the agency will ensure that it is done securely and in compliance with applicable data protection regulations.

  • Confidentiality Obligations

    • All employees and contractors of the agency will be required to sign confidentiality agreements and adhere to strict confidentiality obligations regarding client data.

  • Data Breach Notification

    • In the event of a data breach involving client data, the agency will promptly notify the client and take appropriate steps to mitigate any potential harm.

  • Compliance with Laws

    • The agency agrees to comply with all relevant data protection laws and regulations, including but not limited to GDPR, CCPA, and other applicable privacy laws.

  • Audit Rights

    • The client may have the right to audit the agency's data handling practices to ensure compliance with the agreement and applicable laws.
